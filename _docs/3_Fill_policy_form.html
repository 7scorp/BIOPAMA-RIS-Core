--- title: Fill in the Policy form icon: fa-book order: 3 ---
  • When the pop up opens, you will see it as in image n.1.
    • Attribute the Geographic Scope. In the example for Antigua&Barbuda, it is 'National'.
  • Choose a short and a full name for your policy (e.g.'Antigua and Barbuda NBSAP 2015')
  • Attribute the Category based on the one indicated in the excel sheet (e.g. Management of protected areas). Each Policy listed in the worksheet has one.
  • Choose an icon and description for this policy (optional).
  • In the 'Policy Source' field, add the name of the organisation that reported on this Policy.
  • Copy-paste the link to the official website of the organisation that issued the policy document (optional).
  • Under the 'Reference Document' section, upload the Reference Document (in pdf version) and click 'Upload'.
  • Don't forget to Save!
      • NB: Contents saved as "Pending" will need BIOPAMA staff approval to become public.If you are a Technical Officer, you can save as Public.
