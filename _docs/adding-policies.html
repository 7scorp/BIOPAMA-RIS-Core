--- title: Adding Policies icon: fa-book order: 2 ---
  • Login with your credentials
    • (If you do not have an account, create one first. If you don't know how, have a look at the 'Create an account' documentation on the left)
    • Go on the Action Component web page and click on your BIOPAMA region: the download of an excel file for your region will start automatically (image n.1)
    • Each worksheet contains policy priorities classified as regional (first worksheet) or national (second onwards). Pick up one country,e.g. Antigua&Barbuda, to start (image n.2)
    • Select a policy and copy its text. Go back to the RIS and click on the 'Response' button as shown in the image n.3
    • Choose the scope (image n.4): is this policy reported in a global, regional, national or local document? In this example, we selected the policy from a national worksheet, so the level is national
    • Click the 'Add a policy' button (image n.5)and a pop-up window will open: follow the instructions in the form to add the metadata related to this policy. If you need help, use the 'Filling the form' instructions on the left
    • Don't forget to save!
