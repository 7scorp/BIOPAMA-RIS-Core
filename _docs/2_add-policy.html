--- title: Add a Policy icon: fa-book order: 2 ---
  • Login with your credentials.
    • (If you do not have an account, go on 'Create an account'.
    • Go on the Action Component website and click on your BIOPAMA region: the download of an excel sheet containing your regional, national and/or protected area level policy priorities will automatically begin (image n.1).
    • Policy priorities are targets to achieve within a given time frame. They are associated to a Category, a Reference Document (Policy) and a Geographic Scope (see image below for examples from different worksheets). In the example, a priority for Antigua&Barbuda is 'Better management guidelines'.
    • Select the first in the list and copy its name. Go back to the RIS and click on the 'Response' button as shown in the image n.3
    • Choose the scope (image n.4): is this policy reported in a global, regional, national or local document? In this example, we selected the policy from a national worksheet, so the level is national
    • Click the 'Add a policy' button (image n.5)and a pop-up window will open: follow the instructions in the form to add the metadata related to this policy. If you need help, use the 'Filling the form' instructions on the left
    • Don't forget to save!
