  • Go on the Action Component website to download the excel file for your region (image n.1).
  • Open the file and copy the text of one of the Priorities ('Policies' in the RIS).
    • NB: Each Priority in the document is associated to a Category, a Reference Document and a Geographic Scope(image n.1).
    • In the RIS, click the 'Response' button as shown in image n.3.
    • Choose the Scope (image n.2): is this Policy a global, regional, national or local ?

    In this example, we selected our Policy from a national plan, so the Scope is national(image n.3).

    • Click the 'Add a Policy' button (image n.3) and the Policy form will open in a pop-up window on the right.
    • Follow the instructions in the form to add the Policy in the RIS.

    If you need help, use the next documentation page.
