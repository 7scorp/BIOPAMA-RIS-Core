--- title: Add a Policy icon: fa-book order: 2 ---
  • Go on the Action Component website to download the excel file for your region (image n.1).
  • Open the file and copy the text of one of the Priorities ('Policies' in the RIS).
    • Each Priority is associated to a Category, a Reference Document and a Geographic Scope (image n.2).
    • In the RIS, click the 'Response' button as shown in image n.3.
    • Choose the Scope (image n.4): is this Policy a global, regional, national or local ? In this example, we selected our Policy from a national plan, so the Scope is national
    • Click the 'Add a Policy' button (image n.5) and a pop-up window will open: follow the instructions to add the Policy in the RIS.
      • If you need help, use the next documentation page.
      • Don't forget to save!
          • NB: Contents saved as "Pending" will need BIOPAMA staff approval to become public.
