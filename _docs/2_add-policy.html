--- title: Add a Policy icon: fa-book order: 2 ---
  • Login with your credentials.
    • (If you do not have an account, go on 'Create an account'.
    • Go on the Action Component website and click on your BIOPAMA region: the download of an excel sheet containing your regional, national and/or protected area level policy priorities will automatically begin (image n.1).
    • Each Policy is associated to a Category, a Reference Document and a Geographic Scope (see image below for examples from different worksheets). In the example, a Priority for Antigua&Barbuda is 'Better management guidelines'.
    • Select the first Policy in the list and copy its name. Go back to the RIS and click on the 'Response' button as shown in the image n.3
    • Choose the Scope (image n.4): is this Policy a global, regional, national or local ? In this example, we selected the policy from a national plan, so the level is national
    • Click the 'Add a Policy' button (image n.5) and a pop-up window will open: follow the instructions in the form to add the metadata related to this policy. If you need help, use the next documentation page.
    • Don't forget to save!
    • Worth noticing: if you save as Published, the content will be publicly visible. If you save it as Pending Approval, the content will be visible only to you till the approval of a BIOPAMA technical officer.
